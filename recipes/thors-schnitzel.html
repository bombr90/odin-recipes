Thor's Schnitzel



Description

A scrumptiously tender piece of breaded and fried meat known to restore
even the Defender of Asgard's strength after battle

Thor - the god of Thunder enjoyed battle - so much that he would use his
treasured Mjollnir to tenderize the meat of his prey. We've
scoured ancient Norse texts and procured this recipe for you. Now set forth
and hone your cookery skills so you may feast upon a dish befit for even
Asgard's mightiest warrior.

Ingredients

  • Two boneless pork steaks
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • One large egg (lightly beaten)
  • 1/2 cup panko (or plain bread crumbs)
  • salt and pepper
  • Two cups oil

Steps

  1. Preheat ~1/2" worth of oil in a shallow pan to ~330F.
  2. Place pork steaks between two sheets of parchment paper. Using a meat
    mallet, pound meat until 1/4 inch thick. Lightly season with salt and pepper.
  3. Dredge and coat the flattened morsels of meat by first coating it in flour,
    then dipping it in the beaten egg and finally sprinkling the panko to coat
    it in its entirety.
  4. Fry Schnitzel immediately until coating is deep golden brown (approximately 2-3
    minutes a side).
  5. transfer to cooling rack lined with paper towel to absorb excess oil for a
    few minutes.
  6. Serve immediately with slice of lemon and parsley.
